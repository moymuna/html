About Us

Leading the way in Applied Human Science Education

Our History

Established in 1961, CAHS is the first college in Bangladesh dedicated to home economics and applied human science education.

Our Mission

To provide quality education and research in applied human sciences, preparing students for professional excellence and societal contribution.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in applied human science education, recognized nationally and internationally for academic excellence.
